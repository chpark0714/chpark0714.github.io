Introduction

I am Chol Hyn Park, a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at the University of Nevada, Las Vegas. My research focuses on anonymous cyber threat intelligence sharing on blockchain, integrating zero-knowledge proofs (ZKPs) to enhance security and privacy. With extensive experience in machine learning, cybersecurity, and blockchain, I have contributed to multiple DOE and AFRL-sponsored research projects, including SCADA security, CANBus communication, and multipath TCP for satellite networks.

Throughout my career, I have mentored and taught students at various levels, from university courses to K-12 education. As a teaching assistant and tutor, I have designed and delivered interactive and engaging lessons, fostering a dynamic learning environment. My dedication to education extends beyond the classroom, where I have guided master's and Ph.D. students in cybersecurity research, emphasizing both technical depth and practical application.

I am passionate about teaching and mentoring students, guiding them to become future leaders who will make a significant impact in both industry and academia. Through my experience in cybersecurity, blockchain security, and AI applications, I strive to equip students with the knowledge and skills they need to tackle real-world security challenges. My ultimate goal is to inspire and empower them to contribute meaningfully, whether by driving innovation in the industry or advancing cutting-edge research in academia.

Named Data Network (NDN) Performance Research

  • Conducted research on the architecture and performance of Named Data Networking (NDN), focusing on how its design improves data dissemination and security in network systems.
  • Evaluated NDN's performance metrics, such as data retrieval speed, cache efficiency, and security features, in comparison to traditional IP-based networks.

Reward System using Ethereum ERC-20 Token

  • Designed and implemented a reward system utilizing Ethereum blockchain technology and ERC-20 tokens.
  • Developed a smart contract to issue and manage custom tokens used as rewards for completing specific tasks or objectives.
  • Built a web-based interface for users to earn, track, and redeem tokens.

Real Estate NFT Auction System with ERC-721 Tokens

  • Created a blockchain-based auction system for real estate assets using Ethereum's ERC-721 non-fungible tokens (NFTs).
  • Developed a smart contract to tokenize real estate properties as NFTs, enabling ownership transfer and auction functionalities on the blockchain.
  • Built a user-friendly web interface where users could list properties, place bids, and securely transfer ownership upon auction completion.

Renewable Energy Transaction Platform on Ethereum Blockchain

  • Developed a website facilitating the buying and selling of renewable energy generated from photovoltaic (PV) systems.
  • Implemented an Ethereum-based payment system using ERC-20 tokens for seamless transactions.
  • Ensured all transactions were securely recorded and stored on the blockchain for transparency and immutability.

Smart Greenhouse with AI-Driven Watering System

  • Deployed a sensor board integrated with a Raspberry Pi running Python in a smart greenhouse.
  • Collected environmental data to train an artificial intelligence system for automated watering using the Weka API and Java.
